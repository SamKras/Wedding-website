Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we book wedding cinematography?

I recommend booking 6-12 months in advance for destination weddings, especially for popular dates during peak season (May through October). For local weddings in San Francisco Bay Area, 4-8 months is typically sufficient.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

Yes, I'm available worldwide for destination weddings. I frequently work throughout Europe (Italy, France, Spain, UK), across the USA (California, New York, destination resorts), and international locations. Travel costs are included in custom packages.

What is your cinematography style?

My style is cinematic documentary - authentic, emotional storytelling with artistic composition. I focus on capturing genuine moments as they unfold, working discreetly to document the real emotions, laughter, and tears of your celebration.
